vector<Card> State::cards

The information on all the cards.

This vector also contains information on which card is in what category and which card they are with respect to their category. Consider the following example [card0, ..., card7] and we have a SET_SIZE of 4 and thus a NUM_SETS of 2 then the cards [card0, ..., card3] belong to set/category 0 and [card4, ..., card7] belong to set/category 1. In these examples card4 will be card 0 with respect to its own set/category and card7 will be card 3.
